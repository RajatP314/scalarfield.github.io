Thoughts About the Universe

Here's a thing I just thought about: the current state of the universe is some configuration of things in 3D space. That forms a particular slice of 4D spacetime corresponding to this exact moment. You can then imagine all past and future events as successive space slices arranged along the time direction, creating the 4D manifold of spacetime. Similarly, you could imagine all possible spacetimes, all possible courses of evolution for our universe, to be lined up in a 5D space. If I can get so much out of just 5 dimensions, what would the 6th, or 7th, or higher dimensions entail?
